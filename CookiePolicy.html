What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, enable certain website functionality, and provide website analytics.

Types of cookies

There are two main types of cookies:

  • Session cookies: These cookies are deleted when you close your browser and are used to remember your preferences for the current session.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies are stored on your device and are used to remember your preferences for future visits to the website.

How we use cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your preferences, such as your language and location settings.
  • To enable certain website functionality, such as adding items to your shopping cart.
  • To provide website analytics, such as the number of visitors to our website and the pages they visit.

How to manage cookies

You can manage cookies by adjusting your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website and other websites you visit. For more information on how to manage cookies, please refer to your browser's help documentation.

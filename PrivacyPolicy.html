Privacy Policy

At dscounsell.me, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your personal information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect certain information about your visit using Google Analytics. This may include:

  • Your IP address
  • The date and time you accessed our website
  • The pages you visited on our website
  • The type of browser you used to access our website
  • The device and operating system you used to access our website

We collect this information in order to analyze how our website is being used, improve our website's performance, and provide a better experience for our users.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how our website is being used. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your visit to our website. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. These cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information about you, and we do not have access to any information collected by Google Analytics.

You can learn more about Google Analytics and how it collects and processes data by visiting the Google Analytics website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Analyze how our website is being used
  • Improve our website's performance
  • Provide a better experience for our users

We do not sell or share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on our website.
